Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Manali is a beautiful hill station surrounded by mountains, rivers, and forests. I love the cool climate, breathtaking views, and peaceful atmosphere. It's the perfect place to relax and unwind.


  1. Snowboarding and skiing at Solang Valley
  2. Visiting the Hadimba Temple
  3. Trekking and nature walks
  4. Exploring cafes and local markets
  5. breathtaking views
  • My favorite memory from Manali is sitting by the Beas River at sunset, sipping hot tea, and listening to the calming sound of water flowing.
