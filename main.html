Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Manali is a picturesque hill station located in Himachal Pradesh. It's known for its breathtaking views, cool weather, and adventurous attractions.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Great for skiing and snowboarding
  2. Rohtang Pass – Offers snow-capped mountains
  3. Hadimba Temple – Ancient temple with unique wooden architecture
  4. Jogini Falls – Beautiful waterfall with a trek
  5. Old Manali – Known for its cafés and shopping

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – Traditional steamed bread
  • Trout Fish – Local specialty
  • Thukpa – Spicy noodle soup
  • Babru – Himachali stuffed bread
  • Chha Gosht – Mutton cooked in spiced yogurt gravy
