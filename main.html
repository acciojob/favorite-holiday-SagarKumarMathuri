Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Manali is a picturesque hill station located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Surrounded by majestic mountains and lush valleys, it is known for its natural beauty, adventurous activities, and peaceful atmosphere.

  1. Solang Valley – Perfect for skiing and paragliding.
  2. Hadimba Devi Temple – A unique temple with wooden architecture.
  3. Rohtang Pass – Offers stunning snow-covered landscapes.
  4. Old Manali – Known for its bohemian vibe and cafes.
  5. Jogini Waterfall – A peaceful spot for a scenic trek.
  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Trout Fish – Freshly cooked river fish, a local delicacy.
  • Thukpa – A warm and spicy noodle soup.
  • Babru – Himachali stuffed bread, similar to a kachori.
  • Chha Gosht – A rich and flavorful mutton curry.
