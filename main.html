Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most iconic cities in the world, known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and world-class cuisine. It offers a perfect blend of art, romance, and culture.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A gothic architectural wonder.
  4. Montmartre – A charming hilltop district known for its bohemian vibe.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A scenic boat ride offering views of famous monuments.
  • Croissants – Fresh, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot – A traditional French delicacy of snails with garlic butter.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate almond-based sweets.
  • Baguette – The classic French bread, crispy on the outside, soft inside.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
